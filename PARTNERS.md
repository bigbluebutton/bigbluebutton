# Project Partners & Contributors

This document lists organizations that actively contribute to the development, maintenance, and ecosystem of this software. 
These partners are recognized for their substantial technical or financial support and are eligible to offer official maintenance agreements and invoicing, as outlined in our [FAIRPAY.md](FAIRPAY.md) guidelines.

Organizations listed here provide professional services, including consulting, custom development, integration support, and Service Level Agreements (SLAs). 
If you are deploying this software in a production environment, we encourage you to contact a partner for sustainable support.

## How to engage a Partner

For professional deployment, we recommend reaching out to a partner of your choice from the list above. They can provide:
*   Technical consulting and architecture review
*   Software maintenance packages
*   Custom feature development and integration
*   Formal Service Level Agreements (SLAs)
*   Priority support and troubleshooting

## How to be listed as a Partner

Is your organization actively contributing code, substantial financial support, or critical ecosystem tooling? 
To be considered for addition to this list, please submit a merge request to this repository. 
In your request comment, please clearly describe:
1.  The nature of your contributions (e.g. core code commits, maintained plugins, financial sponsorship).
2.  A link to your organization's website.
3.  The services you offer to users of this software.

Listings are granted at the discretion of the project maintainers to organizations that demonstrate sustained and meaningful support for the project's ecosystem.
