## Pay the Developers

This software has been developed through the considerable effort of its contributors. While free software guarantees your liberty to run, study, modify, and share it (refer to LICENSE), its long-term health depends on a reciprocal ecosystem.

Organizations deploying this software for their staff, students or as a service for their clients are expected to give back to the project. Contributions ensure its maintenance and speeds up developing new features. Your support can be technical, promotional, and financial.

### Fair Budget

For a smmoth operation, we strongly advise organizations to allocate an annual budget for external project support, integration tasks and SLAs.

A financial contribution to the project's foundation or an approved active contributor (listed in PARTNERS.md) is also expected. 
This mutual support is essential to counter the "tragedy of the commons" and ensure the project's viability.

### Recommended Financial Contributions

    One-time fee for medium size production deployment: EUR 5.000.-

    Suggested annual fee per active user: EUR 10.-

These contributions are typically far below the TCO of proprietary software licenses. Organizations that utilize the software without contributing risk damaging their reputation within the community.
